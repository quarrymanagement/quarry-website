FAQ

Common Questions

How much does membership cost?
Rock & Vine membership is $29.99 per month. No setup fees, no annual commitments — cancel anytime.
How do I RSVP for wine tasting events?
Once your profile is submitted and payment is complete, your membership is active. You'll receive a member confirmation email with instructions on how to RSVP for upcoming wine tasting events — members always get access before the general public.
How do I cancel my membership?
You can cancel anytime — two easy options:

Option 1 — Self-service: Go to billing.stripe.com, enter the email you used to sign up, and Stripe will send you a secure link to manage or cancel your subscription instantly.

Option 2 — Email us: Send an email to management@thequarrystl.com and we'll cancel it for you within 24 hours.

Your member access continues until the end of the current billing period.
What happens after I submit the form?
After submitting your profile, you'll be automatically redirected to our secure Stripe checkout page to complete your $29.99/month subscription payment. Once payment is confirmed, your membership is active immediately.
What if my payment fails?
Stripe will automatically retry failed payments. If payment cannot be collected, your membership access will be paused. You'll receive email notifications and can update your payment method at billing.stripe.com.
