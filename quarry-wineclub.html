FAQ

Common Questions

How much does membership cost?
Rock & Vine membership is $29.99 per month. No setup fees, no annual commitments, cancel anytime.
How do I RSVP for wine tasting events?
Once you subscribe and link your membership to your account, you'll see a member portal where active members can RSVP for upcoming events before they open to the public.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes — cancel anytime from your Stripe customer portal or by emailing management@thequarrystl.com. Your access continues through the end of the billing period.
How do I link my subscription to my account?
After subscribing, you'll receive an email with instructions to create or link your account on our site. Once linked, your membership status is verified automatically.
What happens if my payment fails?
Stripe will automatically retry failed payments. If payment cannot be collected, your membership access will be paused until resolved. You'll receive email notifications throughout.
