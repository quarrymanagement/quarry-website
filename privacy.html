Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

The Quarry ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website thequarrystl.com and the restaurant, bar, and event venue located at 3960 Highway Z, New Melle, Missouri 63385. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information you provide to us.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Make a reservation, book a private event, or inquire about weddings or catering
  • Sign up for our email list, wine club, or event notifications
  • Purchase a gift card or event ticket
  • Contact us by phone, email, text, or web form
  • Apply for a position through our careers page

The information we collect may include your name, email address, phone number, mailing address, party size, event details, and any messages you send us.

Payment Information

Online payments (gift cards, ticketed events, deposits) are processed by trusted third-party payment processors such as Stripe and Square. We do not store credit card numbers on our servers. Please review the privacy policies of those providers for details on how they handle your payment data.

How We Use Your Information

  • To confirm and manage reservations, events, and orders
  • To respond to your questions and requests
  • To send you information about upcoming events, live music, specials, and seasonal offerings (only if you opt in)
  • To improve our menus, services, and guest experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies and analytics tools to understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We only share it with service providers that help us operate the business (such as reservation, payment, email, and analytics providers), or when required by law.

Your Choices

You may unsubscribe from our marketing emails at any time using the link in any message we send, or by contacting us directly. You may also request that we update or delete your personal information by emailing management@thequarrystl.com.

Children's Privacy

Our website is intended for a general adult audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
