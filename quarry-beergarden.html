The Vision

The Quarry
Expands

What started as a beloved winery and event destination is growing into something truly extraordinary. The Quarry's 2026 expansion transforms our stunning quarry property into a full outdoor entertainment complex — anchored by a sprawling Beer Garden, live music stage, and open-air pavilions.

Set against the backdrop of our iconic quarry lake and surrounded by nature, this will be a one-of-a-kind destination in the greater St. Louis region — a place where great beer, live music, and unforgettable experiences come together outdoors.
