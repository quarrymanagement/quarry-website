Restaurants Near O'Fallon, MO

O'Fallon has plenty of dinner options. The Quarry isn't one of them — and that's exactly the point.

O'Fallon is the largest city in St. Charles County, and it has the dining scene to match — dozens of national chains, plenty of solid local spots near Renaud Spirit Center and Fort Zumwalt Park, and a thriving everyday restaurant scene. What it doesn't have is much in the way of destination dining. The kind of place you drive to specifically because you want the experience, not just the meal.

The Quarry is that place, and it's only 22 minutes west of O'Fallon. We're a 26-acre lakeside restaurant, wine bar, live music venue, and event destination in New Melle, Missouri. Chef-crafted American food, a serious wine list, indoor golf simulator bays, an outdoor turf and patio area on the water, live entertainment most weekends, and one of the most-booked wedding venues in the county. The drive from O'Fallon is shorter than going downtown, and the parking is free.

Why The Quarry is the 22-minute drive worth making

Here's what we hear from O'Fallon regulars: they tried us once because someone they trusted recommended it, they were skeptical about driving 20-something minutes for dinner, and then they came back the next month. The drive is the part people overthink. Once you've done it, it's a non-issue — the route is straight, the traffic is light past Wentzville, and you're at the property before you've finished your podcast.

The other thing O'Fallon residents tell us: after a while, the chains all blend together. There's nothing wrong with them — they do their job — but when you want a meal that feels like an event, you need a property that's actually built around the experience. We have that. The lake, the patio, the indoor golf bays, the live music, the wine list, the kitchen — all of it works together to make a Friday night at The Quarry feel different from a Friday night anywhere within O'Fallon city limits.

And if you're planning a private party, a corporate dinner, a baby shower, or a wedding — there's nothing in O'Fallon that compares to a 26-acre lakeside venue with a full-service event team. That's a category we own in this part of the county.

How to find us from O'Fallon

From the central O'Fallon area (near CarShield Field, Renaud Spirit Center, or the Fort Zumwalt school district): take Highway K south to I-64 (Highway 40/61) west, exit at Wentzville Parkway, then take Highway Z west the rest of the way. About 22 minutes total.

From the north side of O'Fallon (Ozzie Smith Sports Complex area): take Highway P or Mexico Road west to Wentzville, then jump on Highway Z heading west. Roughly 20 minutes once you're out past the developments.

The route is mostly highway and well-marked country road. No tolls, no complicated transitions, and the last 10 minutes are some of the prettiest driving in St. Charles County.

What you can do at The Quarry on any given visit

What makes The Quarry worth the trip from O'Fallon isn't any one thing — it's that there's almost always something happening. A typical week might include:

  • Wine bar with curated list — Missouri vineyards, regional producers, and small-batch wines you won't find at the chains.
  • Indoor golf simulator bays — six private bays for hourly rental, full food and drink service to the bay, perfect for after-work groups, double dates, or birthday rentals.

  • Sunday brunch with live music — bottomless brunch service, family-friendly until early evening.
