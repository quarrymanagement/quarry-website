How Rewards Work
A few simple rules · No catches
Quarry Rewards
Welcome to the Family
Earn points every visit. Redeem them on food, drinks, and the occasional standing-ovation moment.
Three Steps
1
Earn 10 points per $1
Every dollar at the bar, kitchen, or golf bay earns 10 points automatically when your tab closes. No card to swipe, no code to remember.
2
Hit milestones, get rewards
500 points → $5 off. 750 → free appetizer. 1,500 → $20 toward a wine bottle. Birthday entrée at 2,000. The points don't expire.
3
Bonus points add up
RSVP a public event for +50. Refer a friend who joins for +500. Reach 3,000 lifetime points to unlock Quarry Gold (1.25× earn rate).
The Tiers
★
Quarry Standard
All members start here. 1 point per $1. Full access to rewards.
★★
Quarry Gold · 3,000 lifetime pts
1.25× earn rate. Early access to event RSVPs. Priority on busy nights.
★★★
Quarry Platinum · 6,000 lifetime pts
1.5× earn rate. Hosted tastings invitation. Concierge holiday booking.
Points are earned on closed tabs only and post within 24 hours. Rewards must be redeemed in person at The Quarry. Cannot be combined with other offers. Memberships are free; The Quarry reserves the right to update the program.
