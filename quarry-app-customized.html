How Rewards Work
A few simple rules · No catches
Quarry Rewards
Welcome to the Family
Earn points every visit. Redeem them on food, drinks, and the occasional standing-ovation moment.
Three Steps
1
Earn 10 points per $1
Every dollar at the bar, kitchen, or golf bay earns 10 points based on your pre-tip total (subtotal + tax). Tips go to your server, not Quarry — so they don't earn points. Higher tiers earn at 1.1× to 1.5× the base rate.
2
Redeem from the ladder
500 pts (≈ $50) → free bucket of balls at Surfside · 1,000 (≈ $100) → $10 off your bill · 1,250 (≈ $125) → free glass of wine or beer · 1,500 (≈ $150) → free appetizer · 2,000 (≈ $200) → $20 wine bottle credit · 2,500 (≈ $250) → free entrée + glass · 3,000 (≈ $300) → wine tasting for two · 3,500 (≈ $350) → free bottle. Points never expire.
3
Earn bonus points beyond your tab
Just for stopping in: +10 pts every visit (when your closed tab is $20+). Welcome bonus +250 · Email opt-in +10 · Phone/SMS opt-in +10 · Birthday-month visit +250 · Reservation honored +50 (groups of 10+ included) · Wine tasting RSVP +25 · Refer a friend +50 (cap 5/yr) · Public review +100 · Book a private party +1,000. Live bands are free — listed for what's playing this month, no RSVP needed and no points. Gift cards do not earn points.
Status Tiers · Trailing 90-Day Spend
★
Standard
Welcome to the family. 1.0× earn rate. Full access to the redemption ladder.
★★
Silver · $300 / 90 days (~$100/mo)
1.1× earn rate. The first taste of perks — bottle discounts unlock at Gold.
★★★
Gold · $600 / 90 days (~$200/mo)
1.25× earn rate. 5% off any bottle of wine. Free birthday entrée. Early access to event RSVPs.
★★★★
Elite · $1,000 / 90 days (~$333/mo)
1.5× earn rate. 10% off bottles. One complimentary glass of wine per visit (requires a $20+ closed tab). Priority on private event dates. Named on the wall.
↻
Tier decay — fair, not punitive
If you go 30 days without a visit, we'll send a reminder. After 60 days of zero spend, your tier drops one level. Your points are always safe — only the tier perks decay. A few visits brings the tier right back.
Points are earned on closed tabs only and post within 24 hours. Rewards must be redeemed in person at The Quarry. Cannot be combined with other offers. Memberships are free; The Quarry reserves the right to update the program.
